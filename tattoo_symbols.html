There are three components. Top to bottom:

  1. Aegishjalmer, a protection sigil
  2. Yggdrasil, the worlds tree
  3. Jörmungandr, the world serpent

Givens, by Viking mythology:

  • Yggrassil holds the worlds together
  • Jörmungandr is biting its own tail; if it releases, it is an indicator of a coming Ragnarök (drowning of the world)

Tattoo key points:

  • Yggrassil is on fire
  • A jailed Jörmungandr is at its roots

The rest is left to the reader.
