Highlights

Splits.io

I've been working on my own startup since January 2019.

Statistical analysis

My startup is a statistical analysis company at its core. We take large datasets that users voluntarily upload and give them generated feedback.

Esports

Speedrunning is the track & field of esports, and that's where we're focused. We help speedrunners get faster at games they can already blow through at professional levels.

Commentary

Up next for us is using our large datasets in conglomerate to provide dashboards for commentators and other event staff. Good commentary tells stories, and stories need data.

Twitch

I worked at Twitch from January 2014 to December 2018. I was on several teams before and after acquisition.

Localization

I championed and led a public rework of how we handle localization on Twitch. You can read the project summary at the Twitch blog.

Host mode

One night at 9pm three team members and I received a business-critical work text. Over the next 14 hours we built and launched host mode from scratch, a feature still loved by users today.

Auth

Following a security breach, I formed the Identity team with two coworkers where we replaced our authentication infrastructure with a low-surface-area OpenID Connect implementation, still in service today.

Spam

I built the first spam filter on Twitch, still in operation today. I can't really talk about it because spam filters are one of those things that stop working when you talk about them.

Social merging

When Twitch acquired Curse, a company with social features nearly identical to ours, I merged the code, products, and data powering social features like Friends without downtime for either platform.
